Why Choose Silver Driving School?

Welcome to the Silver Driving school website, my name is Hassan Syed. I am an independent Approved Driving Instructor (ADI). I operate in many areas of Bristol.

Learning to drive is a key skill in life both fun and rewarding. It provides a greater degree of independence and flexibility to social and working life. My job is to teach pupils in a comfortable environment, with understanding and patience at the forefront of my mind. No one pupil is the same therefore I will adapt my teaching style according to how the pupil deals with the lesson to get the best performance out of them.

Our Lesson Structure

Driving lessons are structured to be goal orientated this way the learner always has a yardstick to see if they have improved and by how much on a lesson by lesson basis. I also aim to have a client centred approach, to put simply, people learn in different ways and at different speeds.

For example if someone likes to reflect on their learning is made to move onto the next thing too quickly it could slow their progress down. Or if someone who is given too many demonstrations and gets frustrated because they didn't get to have a go could again slow their progress down.

A Typical Lesson

Recap - in the beginning of each lesson to discuss how the previous lesson went.

Objectives - Carry out objectives set from previous lesson.

Debrief - At the end of each lesson discuss how the lesson went, what went well and what didn't and see if we met these objective/s.

This structure helps eliminate any feeling of aimlessness for the learner and encourages them to reach for targets resulting in more productive lessons.
