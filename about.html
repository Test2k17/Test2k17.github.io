About

Centercomms Ltd expertise and scope covers, but is not limited to,

  • Repair and maintenance of the existing duct networks.
  • Planning and execution of new and start up networks.
  • Chamber construction, and manhole construction.
  • Roadside cabinet erection for BT's high-speed broadband rollout, and new customer connections.
  • Sub-ducting and fibre optic cable blowing capabilities.

All works team undertake their own rigid and modular surface reinstatements and carry the appropriate street works qualifications in order to do so.

The Centercomms Support and Supervision team are particularly well versed in the street works and utility arena and are expert in the administration of the NRSWA and the TMA specifically in the Permit Scheme areas of London and the Home Counties.

The Management Team are committed to a controlled and efficient growth in the Utilities Market, and have already achieved a measure of success since 2007, with further sustainable growth planned for the foreseeable future.

'The Centercomms Ltd Team' are a young and enthusiastic group who are easy to deal with and are known for their professionalism and first-class customer service.

A worthy Partner in your next venture.

Christopher Flanagan
Managing Director

Chris formed Centercomms Ltd in 2007 from a successful and growing subcontracting business which he ran for a number of years in the Midlands and London areas. His vision for Centercomms Ltd was to build a new organisation to compete as a tier 1 or tier 2 supplier, focussed on quality of service, and be the provider of choice for the telecoms and utilities sectors of the civil engineering market.

With over 40 years' experience in the industry Chris remains as the principal mover and shaker of the business, and provides support for the focus on growth and performance of the Business as a whole.

Bob Chambers
General Manager

An Operations Director with extensive National and International experience in construction and project management in the fields of Civil Engineering, Utilities and Telecommunications infrastructure and facilities. His telecoms experience is founded on the successful roll out of pan European fibre networks, new city fibre builds in continental Europe, Asia, and Australasia, and data centre construction in some of the financial capitals of Europe.

Bob joined Centercomms Ltd in 2009 as General Manager, and is currently responsible for the Business & Operational Management of Centercomms Ltd.

Marinella Bogescu
Finance Manager

Mari joined Centercomms Ltd in 2008 and has been an integral part of the Company's successful growth into a supplier of choice within the telecoms and utilities sector.

She is an extremely motivated and focused accountant with twenty-four years' experience in financial reporting and accountancy. A broad and comprehensive knowledge of financial procedures and a keen eye for detail leads to fluid and transparent book-keeping and accounting. With a positive an forward-thinking attitude combined with a calm, professional demeanour, she delivers detailed and accurate accounting results to the management team.
